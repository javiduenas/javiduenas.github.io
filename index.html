 PHILOSOPHY OF TEACHING & LEARNING

Growth cannot happen without change, and learning cannot happen without a personal commitment to change. This also requires humility: the ability to accept one's mistakes and grow because of (sometimes despite) them. Although learning can feel very solitary, especially in online learning environments, it is a fundamentally social activity, transferring ideas, emotions, and experiences to other people through personal, professional, societal, and cultural networks, using one of the earliest forms of educational technology: Language.
