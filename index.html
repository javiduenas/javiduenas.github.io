 PHILOSOPHY OF TEACHING & LEARNING

Growth cannot happen without change, and the growth that comes from learning cannot happen without a personal commitment to making that change within oneself. The nature of the change and the degree of change can be influenced by many factors, but the most important one is a sincere commitment to change. The second is humility: the ability to accept one's mistakes and grow because of (sometimes despite) them.

Although learning can feel very solitary at times, especially in online learning environments, it is a fundamentally social activity, transferring ideas, emotions, and experiences through personal, professional, societal, and cultural networks, and often using some of the earliest forms of educational technology: Language and writing.
